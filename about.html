ABOUT

Ulan-Ulan Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder nestled in the lush landscapes of Biliran Island, Philippines. The name "Ulan-Ulan," which means "rain" in the local dialect, aptly describes the falls as its cascading waters create a mist that feels like gentle rain, enhancing its enchanting atmosphere. Surrounded by verdant forests and rugged terrain, Ulan-Ulan Falls offers a serene escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The trek to the falls involves navigating through scenic trails, crossing streams, and enjoying the vibrant biodiversity of the area, making it an ideal destination for eco-tourism. Visitors are rewarded with the sight of the powerful yet graceful flow of water tumbling into a crystal-clear basin, perfect for refreshing swims and photo opportunities. The falls serve not only as a tourist attraction but also as a testament to the natural beauty and ecological richness of Biliran. Its unspoiled environment invites travelers to experience the harmony of nature and embrace the tranquility of this hidden gem.
